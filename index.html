Our Story

Fourteen Years of Flavor, Family & Fire

Saffron & Stone was born in 2012 from a single, deeply held belief — that great Indian food deserves to be presented with the same reverence it's prepared with. Founded by the Mehta family on the quiet lanes of Banjara Hills, what began as an intimate 30-seat dining room has grown into one of Hyderabad's most beloved culinary destinations.

Every dish on our menu traces its lineage to a specific place and time — the dum biryani to the royal kitchens of Hyderabad, the rogan josh to the valleys of Kashmir, the dal makhani to a recipe handwritten by our grandmother in 1967. We source our saffron from Iran, our spices directly from Kochi, and our dairy from a single farm in Chittoor.

When you dine with us, you are not simply having a meal. You are entering a relationship built on decades of craft, hospitality, and an unwavering commitment to making every guest feel like family. That is the promise of Saffron & Stone — and it begins the moment you walk through our doors.

"Every plate carries the memory of someone's home."
